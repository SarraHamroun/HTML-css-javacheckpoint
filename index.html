Creation of Netflix


Netflix was launched in 1998. At that point, there was no HD technology nor streaming culture. Yet the pursuit of comfort and accessibility was in fact what sprung the business idea. Let us talk history; in 1996, Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph were struck by the emergence of the e-commerce phenomenon in general, and the birth of Amazon in particular. They then wanted to reinterpret its business idea of home delivery and bring it to a field where they perceived an upcoming market demand. That field being home entertainment. Until that point, people would rent movies on VHS from brick-and-mortar stores. So when the DVD was invented in 1996, the two entrepreneurs took advantage of the lightweight of the discs which would not cost much to be shipped to users.
